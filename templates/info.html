Q: How does the algorithm work?

A: The algorithm takes into account two parameters.

Priorities: It is ranked from 1 to 5, where 1 is the most important and 5 is the least.

Predecessor: A task can have a single predecessor, meaning this task must be achieved before scheduling the next optimization time.

It is important to consider that the solution provided by this algorithm is only a suggestion; there could be more than one single optimal solution.

The resources parameter is only there as a personal note and is not taken into account for the algorithm.

Q: How to manage my task?

A: To create a task go directly on the dashboard, click on add task and fill the form correctly

To delete a task you can simply click on the dashboard on the delete button near to the task you want to delete

Currently the application doesn't provide a solution to modify existing task, so you might have to delete and recreate a task to modify it

Q: Can I modify my Login?
A: For now you doesn't have the possibility to modify your login
